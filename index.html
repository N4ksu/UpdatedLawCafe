About Us

Law Café is a unique coffee shop owned by a licensed lawyer. When you order coffee, you get a free legal consultation with access to basic legal forms. We serve high-quality coffee made from premium beans, bold, rich, and perfect for any day.

Our website lets you book a table online by simply filling out a reservation form. Law Café is where great coffee meets free legal advice, all in one place.
